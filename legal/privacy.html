This Privacy Policy describes how ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit or use our professional training platform at . By using our platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

01 Overview

We operate a privacy-conscious online professional training platform specialising in HR, PMP, and IT certification preparation. Our goal is to collect only the minimum information necessary to deliver courses, process purchases, and provide support. We do not sell your data, build advertising profiles, or share your information with third-party marketers.

Our platform is a static website. There is no user account system, no registration form, and no persistent login portal. Access to purchased course content is managed through a simple, secure per-course credential system.

02 Data We Collect

2.1 — Information You Provide

We collect your email address when you purchase a course. This email is used solely to create your course access credentials, which are managed manually in a private, access-controlled Google Sheet.

No payment information (credit cards, bank details, etc.) is ever collected or stored by us. All purchase discussions happen directly via WhatsApp, and payment is arranged personally between you and us.

2.2 — Information Collected Automatically

When you submit a course rating, our server-side gateway records your IP address for the sole purpose of preventing duplicate ratings for the same course. This IP address is stored in a private Google Sheet alongside your rating and is never used for tracking, profiling, or any other purpose.

Standard server logs may be generated by our hosting provider (Cloudflare) and may include IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps. These logs are handled according to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy and are not accessible to or used by us for profiling.

2.3 — Information We Do Not Collect

  • Full name (not required for purchase or access)
  • Phone number (WhatsApp contact is initiated by you)
  • Payment card or banking details
  • Social media profiles or login tokens
  • Device fingerprints or tracking pixels
  • Professional certification IDs or employer information
  • Location data beyond IP-level country detection by Cloudflare

03 How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Course access provisioning: Your email address is used to create a unique set of login credentials that grant you access to the specific HR, PMP, or IT course(s) you purchased.
  • Purchase support: We may reference your email when responding to access issues or refund requests submitted via WhatsApp or email.
  • Rating integrity: Your IP address is used to ensure each user can submit only one rating per course, maintaining the accuracy of our course ratings.
  • Platform improvement: Aggregated, anonymised usage data (provided by Cloudflare's infrastructure) may inform decisions about platform performance and training content.

We do not use your data for targeted advertising, email marketing campaigns, or any automated decision-making processes.

04 Content Storage & Google Drive

All course content — training videos, study guides, practice exams, and supplementary materials — is hosted on Google Drive, a service provided by Google LLC. Course files are stored in private, access-restricted folders. Direct links to course content are never exposed to the browser; they are handled server-side by our API gateway and only served after successful authentication.

Your course access credentials (email and password) are stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to authorised administrators. Google's storage infrastructure is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Course content URLs are never stored in frontend code, browser storage, or any client-accessible location. Access is enforced server-side on every request.

05 Cookies & Local Storage

We use a minimal cookie policy. Specifically, we set only one type of cookie — an access cookie issued when you successfully log in to a purchased course.

Cookie details
Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
__paid_{courseId} Grants access to a specific purchased course. Contains a cryptographically signed token with your course ID, email, and expiry time. 30 days HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Strict

This cookie is:

  • HttpOnly — not accessible to JavaScript running on the page, protecting against cross-site scripting attacks.
  • Secure — transmitted only over HTTPS connections.
  • SameSite=Strict — not sent with cross-site requests, protecting against cross-site request forgery.
  • Path-scoped — restricted to /course/paid/{courseId}, meaning the cookie for one course cannot be used to access a different course.

We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party cookie services. We do not use localStorage or sessionStorage for storing personal information.

07 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy:

  • Course access credentials (email + password): Retained for the lifetime of your course access. If you request deletion of your access or a full refund, your credentials are removed from our records within 7 business days.
  • Rating data (IP + rating value): Retained indefinitely as part of the course rating history. If you request removal of your rating, we will delete the associated record upon verification.
  • Access cookie: Expires automatically after 30 days. You may delete it at any time through your browser settings.
  • WhatsApp messages: Governed by WhatsApp's own retention policies. We do not export or archive your WhatsApp messages to any external system.

08 Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure:

  • All traffic is served exclusively over HTTPS — enforced by Cloudflare.
  • Course access cookies are cryptographically signed using HMAC-SHA256, preventing tampering or forgery.
  • Course content URLs are never exposed to the browser — they are fetched and served entirely server-side.
  • A strict Content Security Policy (CSP) is applied to every page, preventing injection attacks.
  • API endpoints are protected by per-IP rate limiting enforced at the Cloudflare Worker layer.
  • All dynamic content on the platform is built using the DOM API — no user data is ever injected as raw HTML, eliminating a class of XSS vulnerabilities.

While we take all reasonable precautions, no system is completely immune to security risks. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you via the email address associated with your course access as promptly as reasonably possible.

09 Your Rights

Regardless of your location, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data held by us:

  • Right to access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may request that we correct inaccurate data (e.g., a misspelled email address).
  • Right to erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data. Note that deleting your access credentials will revoke your course access.
  • Right to restrict processing: You may request that we limit how we use your data while a request or dispute is being resolved.
  • Right to data portability: You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 12 — Contact Us. We will respond within 30 days.

10 Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for working professionals and adult learners pursuing HR, project management, and IT certifications. It is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove that information from our records.
